Our Story

Youth Empowerment Advocate (YEA) is a movement built by young people, for young people. We exist to inspire, educate, and empower the next generation to rise above challenges, unlock their potential, and become changemakers in their communities and beyond.

At YEA, we believe that empowerment starts with a shift in mindset. By creating a platform where voices are heard, ideas are shared, and opportunities are made accessible, we aim to bridge the gap between today’s challenges and tomorrow’s solutions.

This is more than a movement, it is a community. Every youth who joins adds strength, vision, and energy to a collective force dedicated to growth, equity, and sustainable impact. Together, we are not just dreaming of a better future, we are building it.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every young person is equipped with the mindset, resources, and network to thrive. YEA exists to amplify youth voices and unlock their leadership potential for a more inclusive and impactful tomorrow.
