Meet Our Founders

The passionate voices behind Youth Empowerment Advocate (YEA)

Atta Owoahene Acheampong

Founder · Ghana

I am passionate about helping young people break free from limiting beliefs and step into growth. As a writer, I’ve published two books: Shifting Mindset (released free to inspire safety-conscious living) and A Journey Through the Inner Conflict, currently available on Amazon. Through YEA, I aim to mentor, connect, and empower youth to achieve their highest potential.

Ms Sadi

Co-Founder · Pakistan

A dedicated writer and advocate for mindset transformation. One of her renowned works, Mindset Reset, has inspired many in reshaping their perspectives. As co-founder of YEA, she brings global collaboration and a fresh voice to our mission, ensuring young people across borders are empowered to lead and innovate.

Message from Our Founders
